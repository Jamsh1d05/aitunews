Seminar on combating corruption in the academic environment

On November 2, 2022, within the walls of AITU, as part of the "Clean Session" campaign for students of 1-2 courses, a seminar was held on the topic: "Basic principles and methods of combating corruption in the academic environment." The speaker of the seminar was Mamyt Aidos Zhanuzakovich, Officer for Particularly Important Cases of the Prevention Department of the Department of the Anti-Corruption Agency of the Republic of Kazakhstan for the city of Astana.

Memorandum of cooperation with the Civil Alliance of Kazakhstan

On October 19, a meeting of the expert commission "Digital transformation of Kazakhstan in the context of global competition" was held with the participation of AITU Rector Ahmed-Zaki D.Zh and President of the Civil Alliance of Kazakhstan Nurgaziyeva B.G. The parties signed a memorandum of cooperation. Also at the meeting, the Director of the Department for Analysis and Automation of Business Processes of the Corporate Fund "University Medical Center" Mavlenov S.A., General Director of the Association of Legal Entities "Association of Digital Transformation" Ostapyuk S.V. made a report.
